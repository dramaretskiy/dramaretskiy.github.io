Celebrating 20 Years of Quality Service

YOU.

Crilly Lawyers is a small practice of dedicated legal professionals with a wide skill set and diverse knowledge. Building good relationships is our priority.

We want to get to know you and your business so we can provide excellent legal advice.

We want to help you buy your first house, manage your business interests and when the time comes, help with your retirement.

We want to understand you so we can minimize and manage your legal risk and exceed your service expectations.

We will talk to you in a language you understand and we will never recommend something we would not do ourselves.

The Crilly Difference

AVAILABILITY

At other firms, you will only ever speak to a partner or senior lawyer, while all the work gets done by junior lawyers or support staff. This means that you might never talk to the people that are actually working for you.

Our lawyers maintain direct contact with you at all times during the course of your matter, so you always know who’s working for you.

For busy clients we can offer out of hours meetings to enable estate planning and other documents to be discussed and signed. Just ask us how and we can make ourselves available when you need.

SERVICES

Many firms will only work on a single matter, or type of matter, for you, and miss the other issues which may affect you in other areas.

The broad experience of our team ensures that we are able to handle all the issues that you may encounter in your business or personal life.

LOCAL

International firms have established a strong foothold and are taking over many Australian firms. Many firms are also outsourcing legal work to other countries. Are you sure you know who has access to your personal and confidential information and where it’s stored?

We are firmly an Australian firm. All your personal information is stored locally with our Australian service providers.
