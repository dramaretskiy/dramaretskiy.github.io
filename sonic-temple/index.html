The Sonic Temple is a state-of-the-art recording studio that upholds the ideals of artistic and technical excellence. It’s a facility that truly has few rivals in the world.

Surrounded by 24.03 beautiful acres

The Sonic Temple sits upon a private mountain surrounded by 24.03 beautiful acres. A private, gated entrance at the base of the mountain leads up a scenic, tree-lined drive. During this three-minute car ride, guests are likely to pass a deer or two meandering in the meadow. As daylight passes through the countless fir trees and rugged pines, it’s easy to be awed by this property’s natural majesty.
